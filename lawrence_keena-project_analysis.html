Project Analysis

Site Intent

The objective of the Christ Episcopal Church website redesign is to provide structural organization and focus on the usability of the target audience. I was also able to find that there are 52 errors and 8 warnings when I ran the HTML code through two validators. The code will need to be cleaned up as well. Organization and usability will be achieved by adding navigation to the site, cleaning up the basic structure of the site and reducing some content.

Competitive Analysis

Taking a look at Christianity and Church related sites, there is a high rate of competition out there. It is mainly a competition of the different denominations and Church locations. I was able to find that more sites in this category are getting more hits a day, customers are staying on their page longer and they are pulling in the majority of the 25-34 year old audience.

Key Words and Search Queries

Keywords and search queries that are on the rise and generating more traffic for this site and it's local competition are: Seattle christ church and christian churches in seattle. "Churches in seattle" is currently the most popular search query.

Targeted Audience

I wasn't able to find any specific information on the audience from the site from alexa.com, but I was able to analyze other sites in this category and some of the competition. The average vistor is in the 25-34 year old age range. Most of the visitors are typically women. I would expect that as far as workflow goes, these visitors are here to either learn about the congregation because they are looking for a new church to attend or they are already members of the church. Members of the church would be most likely looking for information such as church event information, using the directory to find fellow church members telephone numbers or to check up on church news and prayer lists. New visitors would be looking for church service hours, information about the church and services, location and directions and possibly contact information to get in touch with the pastor.
